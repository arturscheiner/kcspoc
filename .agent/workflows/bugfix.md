---
description: Fix a bug in a stable kcspoc release.
---

Steps:
1. Ask for the affected version.
2. Identify the minimal safe fix.
3. Ensure no behavior change beyond the fix.
4. Suggest a CHANGELOG.md entry under the next patch version.
5. Indicate whether the fix should be propagated to main.

Constraints:
- No refactoring beyond what is strictly necessary.
