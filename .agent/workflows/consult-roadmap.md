---
description: Consult Roadmap
---
Summarize the current roadmap and assess alignment of a proposed idea or change.

Steps:
1. Identify the relevant roadmap document(s).
2. Summarize the applicable goals and constraints.
3. Assess whether the proposal aligns with:
   - current stabilization (0.5.x),
   - development roadmap (0.6.x),
   - or future capabilities.
4. Highlight risks if the proposal is misaligned.

Output format:
- Applicable roadmap section
- Alignment status (Aligned / Misaligned / Unclear)
- Short justification
